Prodigious Makers

At least a song a day

Robert Ellsworth Pollard Jr. (born October 31, 1957)[1] is an American musician and singer-songwriter who is the leader and creative force behind indie rock group Guided by Voices. In addition to his work with Guided by Voices, he continues to have a prolific solo career with 22 solo albums released so far.

With more than 2,000 songs registered to his name with BMI,[2] Pollard is among the most prolific songwriters of his time. In 2006, Paste magazine listed him as the 78th greatest living songwriter.[3] In 2007, he was nominated for the Shortlist Music Prize.
